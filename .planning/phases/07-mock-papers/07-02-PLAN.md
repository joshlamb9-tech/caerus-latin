**Gap areas (per D-16), each as a `.gap-card`:**

1. **Passive Voice**
   - CE knows: "Active voice across all 4 conjugations and all tenses"
   - GCSE adds: "Full passive voice paradigms &mdash; present, imperfect, future, perfect, pluperfect, and future perfect passive for all conjugations"
   - Link: `<a href="grammar/passive.html" class="gap-link">Study the passive voice &rarr;</a>`

2. **Subjunctive Mood**
   - CE knows: "Indicative mood only"
   - GCSE adds: "Present, imperfect, and pluperfect subjunctive &mdash; active and passive &mdash; used in purpose clauses, result clauses, indirect commands, and more"
   - Link: `<a href="grammar/subjunctive.html" class="gap-link">Study the subjunctive &rarr;</a>`

3. **Deponent Verbs**
   - CE knows: "Not covered at CE level"
   - GCSE adds: "Verbs that are passive in form but active in meaning &mdash; conor, hortor, loquor, morior, proficiscor, and others on the DVL"
   - Link: `<a href="grammar/deponents.html" class="gap-link">Study deponent verbs &rarr;</a>`

4. **Gerunds and Gerundives**
   - CE knows: "Not covered at CE level"
   - GCSE adds: "The verbal noun (gerund) and verbal adjective (gerundive), including the gerundive of obligation construction"
   - Link: `<a href="grammar/gerunds.html" class="gap-link">Study gerunds &amp; gerundives &rarr;</a>`

5. **Extended Pronouns**
   - CE knows: "Basic personal pronouns (ego, tu, nos, vos) and is/ea/id"
   - GCSE adds: "Full paradigms for hic/haec/hoc, ille/illa/illud, qui/quae/quod, and ipse/ipsa/ipsum"
   - Link: `<a href="grammar/pronouns.html" class="gap-link">Study pronouns &rarr;</a>`

6. **4th and 5th Declension**
   - CE knows: "1st, 2nd, and 3rd declension nouns"
   - GCSE adds: "4th declension (-us, -us) and 5th declension (-es, -ei) noun paradigms"
   - Link: `<a href="grammar/declensions.html" class="gap-link">Study 4th &amp; 5th declension &rarr;</a>`

7. **Syntax Constructions (10 types)**
   - CE knows: "Simple sentence structures, basic subordinate clauses"
   - GCSE adds: "Indirect statement, purpose clauses, result clauses, indirect commands, indirect questions, ablative absolute, temporal/causal/concessive clauses, conditionals, fearing clauses, and participle usage"
   - Link: `<a href="syntax/index.html" class="gap-link">Explore all 11 syntax pages &rarr;</a>`

8. **Translation Technique**
   - CE knows: "Short passage translation at CE level"
   - GCSE adds: "Longer unseen passages at exam level, plus English-to-Latin composition"
   - Link: `<a href="translation/passages.html" class="gap-link">Practise unseen passages &rarr;</a>` and `<a href="translation/english-to-latin.html" class="gap-link">Practise English to Latin &rarr;</a>`

   - Link: `<a href="literature/pete.html" class="gap-link">Learn PETE method &rarr;</a>` and `<a href="literature/index.html" class="gap-link">All literature tools &rarr;</a>`
