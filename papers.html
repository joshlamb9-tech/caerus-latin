How to use past papers

  • Complete under timed conditions (Level 1: 45 min · Level 2: 60 min · Level 3: 75 min)
  • Mark using the mark scheme — award full marks for correct answers phrased differently
  • Review vocabulary or grammar you couldn’t recall using the Vocabulary and Grammar pages
  • Retry the Exercises on topics where you dropped marks
